Single 16:9 screen. All scoreboard data forced to a hard-right rail (class info merged in); left side is a video / sponsor / graphics surface. For venues with 16:9 LED panels.
Layout C — Devon ultra-wide (3:1)
⎯⎯ 6m × 2m ⎯⎯
True Devon aspect (6m × 2m = 3:1). All data fills the full ultra-wide horizontally — no side rails, just three zones: identity left, headline names center, clock + stats right. Class info as a small footer strip.
